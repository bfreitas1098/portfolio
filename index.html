About Me

I'm a frontend developer focused on building responsive, user-centered web applications using React, JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS/SCSS. I enjoy combining design and development to create interfaces that are both visually engaging and technically well structured. In addition to frontend development, I have experience working with Python, Node, REST APIs, Git, and UI/UX design tools like Figma, and I enjoy continuously expanding my skills through personal projects and collaboration with the developer community and previously served as a Leader of Learning at Women Who Code, where I hosted JavaScript study groups and mentored new developers.
